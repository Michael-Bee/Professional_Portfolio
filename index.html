Who is Michael?

A full-stack web developer with a background in design.

I attended the University of Denver's intensive coding boot camp after previously earning a B.F.A. in Graphic Design from Columbia College Chicago. My main focus these days is learning new programing languages to add functionality and value to projects.

I am happiest when when flexing my creative problem-solving chops in making engaging digital experiences. When not slinging code, I enjoy making art through traditional media, gardening, cooking, and playing guitar.

I am available for freelance projects to bring your ideas to life, as well as searching for that web developer dream job.
